Projects

  1. Sentiment Analysis of customer ProductReviews using Machinelearninig

    Project

    Sentiment analysis of customer product reviews is a popular application of machine learning in the field of natural language processing. The objective of this project is to classify customer reviews into positive, negative, or neutral sentiments based on the text data. The project involves several steps such as data collection, data preprocessing, feature extraction, and model training. In the data collection phase, customer reviews of the product are gathered from various sources such as e-commerce websites, social media platforms, and review websites. Data preprocessing involves cleaning and prepping the data by removing unwanted symbols, punctuations, and stop words. Feature extraction involves transforming text data into numerical features that can be understood by the machine learning model. Techniques such as bag-of-words, TF-IDF, and word embeddings are used for feature extraction. The output of the model is a sentiment score for each customer review. Positive scores indicate positive sentiments, negative scores indicate negative sentiments, and scores close to zero indicate neutral sentiments. Overall, sentiment analysis of customer product reviews using machine learning is a useful application that helps businesses understand customer sentiments toward their products and make data-driven decisions to improve customer satisfaction
